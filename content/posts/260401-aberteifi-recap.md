The Grassland ID workshops, organised by [Ceredigion Nature Partnership](https://naturceredigion.cymru) in 2025 were a great success, and showed a fantastic working partnership between the [Ysgol Gynradd Aberteifi](https://ygaberteifi.co.uk/), [Friends of Netpool Park](https://www.facebook.com/groups/842965730705207/), [West Wales Biodiversity Information Centre](https://www.wwbic.org.uk/) and the Partnership. 

What has inspired [Stori Wyllt](https://storiwyllt.cymru/) in addition to the great work at Netpool Park has been the collaboration between the school, [Wales & West Housing Assocation](https://www.wwha.co.uk/) and [Cardished](https://cardished.cymru/) to create wildlife habitat with residents and students. WWHA received a [Small Grant](https://naturceredigion.cymru/en/funding/small-grant-scheme/) from the Partnership for wildlife enhancements across their Ceredigion properties, and this included the creation of bug hotels with the students from Ysgol Gynradd Aberteifi and the residents of [Llys Owen](https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/525723799), with a workshop run by Harry from Cardished. 

[Cardished](https://cardished.cymru/) are an Aberteifi-based charity working with a whole range of Cardigan residents, using local and recycled wood for a whole range of projects. They have recently teamed up with Ceredigion Nature Partnership to build nest boxes, bat boxes. bug hotels and bee hotels. Their workshop at Llys Owen was a major success, and the students proved particularly swift at assembling the hotels!
