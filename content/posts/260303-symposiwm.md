#### Croeso i'r Stori Wyllt symposiwm!<br> Welcome to the Stori Wyllt symposium!

This will be an opportunity to meet the other practitioners on the project, and there'll be a wildlife ID recording session with Carys May from West Wales Biodiversity Information Centre for teachers and artists.
### Itinerary  
1. Croeso _4pm-4.15pm_
2. Wildlife ID recording _4.15pm-5.15pm_
3. Stori Wyllt outline _5.15pm-5.30pm_
4. Workshop structure _5.30pm-6pm_
5. Introductions & buffet _6pm-7pm_

Just to recap, there are:
- 4 schools
- 5x 2-hour workshops **Mar-Jun**
- Group sizes of 12-15 students
- Group ages of Years 4, 5 & 6 (8 to 11)
