## Pip Lewis
#### Working with *Mari Roberts* at [Ysgol Gynradd Aberteifi](/schools/aberteifi/) & *[Yusef Samari](/naturalists#yusef-samari)*
Pip is an artist who lives near Aberteifi/Cardigan, and is a director of [spacetocreate](https://www.spacetocreate.org.uk/), a community interest company supporting people and communities through participation in the visual arts. She has beem involved in the  [Llais yr Afon project](https://www.spacetocreate.org.uk/projects/llais-yr-afon/).
## Molly Gilbert
#### Working with *Cally Mason* at [Ysgol Llanarth](/schools/llanarth/) & *[Jo Dainty](/naturalists#jo-dainty)*
After a life in inner city Bristol working in healthcare, 4 years ago Molly found herself in West Wales looking after Portuguese horses and managing a small field for growing food and as safe space for nature. A passion for all things wild was inspired in her by her mother and it has been her greatest interest, joy and sustenance through all stages of her life. She has kept  journals for most of her adult  life -- adding sketches, watercolour paintings, musings, poems -- and many of these entries reflect her passion for the world around her.
