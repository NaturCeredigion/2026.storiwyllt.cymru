The sessions will provide opportunities to:
- Explore and experiment with a range of art techniques including rubbing, observational drawing, stencilling, collage, wax resist, tracing, pattern-making
- Learn about the birds, moths and butterflies associated with oak trees
- Learn about patterns in nature
- Create a set of observational drawings of leaves
- Create a set of composite images of birds, moths and butterflies associated with oak trees
